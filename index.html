RiceCare

Mobile App for Determining Rice Leaf Color and Precision Nitrogen Fertilizer Estimation

About RiceCare

RiceCare is a mobile app designed to help farmers optimize nitrogen fertilizer use in rice cultivation. By analyzing the color of rice leaves, the app estimates the nitrogen requirements for the crops, ensuring efficient fertilizer application, reducing wastage, and maximizing yield. The app uses advanced image processing techniques to assess leaf color and provides actionable insights.

Features

  • Real-time leaf color analysis using smartphone cameras
  • Estimates precise nitrogen fertilizer requirements based on rice leaf health
  • Helps reduce excess fertilizer use and environmental impact
  • Easy-to-use interface for farmers with minimal technical knowledge
  • Helps increase rice yield by optimizing nitrogen use

Benefits

RiceCare empowers farmers to make informed decisions about fertilizer application. By using the app, farmers can:

  • Save costs on fertilizer by applying the right amount at the right time.
  • Improve environmental sustainability by reducing fertilizer runoff.
  • Enhance rice crop health and yield through optimized nitrogen management.
